disney movie club

Client :Disney Home Entertainment

C.D./A.D. :Henry Chassagniac
Blake Killian

Agency :Zehnder Communications

Team :Dante Nicolas, Dan Carlson

Released :Spring 2015

Disney needed a social strategy that helped reinvigorate an existing community of Disney Movie Club members. We used the content that these fans love most - Disney movies - to keep everyone excited about new DMC releases.

With the help of stellar copywriters and social media strategists, I inundated myself with exisiting Disney Home Entertainment production artwork to choose the right images to match the content calendar we created. For three months, I became the go-to designer to match expertly written copy with a scene or image that conveyed supporting ideas. I also created some new content, in the form of I-Spy style photo manipulation.

Pretty cool, huh?

If you like this and want to chat about doing something like this with me, please get in touch!

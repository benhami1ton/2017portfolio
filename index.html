about Ben

More on LinkedIn
I'm a freelance designer currently residing in Nashville, TN with my beautiful wife and ridiculously handsome rescue dog.

Since graduating from Union University with a degree in Digital Media Studies, I've had the opportunity to work in-house at Krispy Kreme Doughnut Corporation and at the award-winning Zehnder Communications. I love using graphic design as a problem-solving tool and I'm passionate about simple and strategic solutions that look fantastic.

Junior Designer

Designing for national and regional brands based mostly in the Southeast United States. At Zehnder I assisted art directors and senior designers in healthcare, QSR, and beverage industries.

Summer Intern

Two super sweet summers as the marketing intern in Winston Salem, NC. There was lotss of internal design work, with a bit of international Limited Time Offer campaigns sprinkled in.

Throughout college and after graduating I've worked as a freelance designer, with most of my time spent designing typefaces and gig posters. Under the arm of Moonshine Print House, I got to work with some great bands and venues to make promotional materials for two concert seasons.

Digital Media Studies

I studied Digital Media for four years in Jackson, TN, emphasising on Communications and Graphic Design. While at school I served two years as the AIGA student group president and taught myself serigraphy when I wasn't working hard on design and programming homework.
